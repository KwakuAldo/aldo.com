About Aldo Efui Adabunu (Aldo Kwaku Adabunu)

I am Aldo Efui Adabunu, also known as Aldo Kwaku Adabunu, a confident self-taught web developer and system administrator based in Ghana, with strong inter-personal communication skills and a drive to succeed. My expertise spans modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Bootstrap, and TailwindCSS, as well as comprehensive system administration and networking skills.


With over 10 years of experience in IT, including roles at KNUST Network Operation Center and Technology Distributions, I bring a unique perspective to web development projects. I have successfully completed multiple Frontend Mentor challenges, built a Netflix clone as part of the Slightly Techie Cohort 4 program, and currently teach ICT to primary and junior high students.


My specialties include creating responsive and user-friendly websites, Windows OS management (XP through Windows 10 and Server), hardware and software troubleshooting, network setup for homes and businesses, and comprehensive computer repair services. I am passionate about continuous learning and delivering exceptional technical solutions.
